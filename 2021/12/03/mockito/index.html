Wildcard

Conclusion of Wildcard:

when you need to implement when(simpleService.method1(anyInt(), "Jinondo", anyCollection(), isA(Serializable.class))).thenReturn(100);

just need both wildcard(use any to match) and specific value to match

you should use Mockito.eq()

like: when(simpleService.method1(anyInt(), Mockito.eq("Jinondo"), anyCollection(), isA(Serializable.class))).thenReturn(200);
